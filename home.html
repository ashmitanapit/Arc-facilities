Welcome to ARC Facilities

ARC Facilities offers property maintenance and repairs services for building owners and business managers in London and environ. We provide a single point of contact for all building repairs, maintenance, and compliance requirements. We provide diverse range of property maintenance services in a timely, efficient, professional, and cost-effective manner. By removing the distraction of building maintenance, businesses are thus able to concentrate on the most important thing, that is running their business. Our services include mechanical and electrical services; heating, ventilation and air-conditioning services, carpentry, painting and decoration, plaster boarding, tiling, small fits and repairs of furniture and equipment. So Contact us for property maintenance service or any other kinds of services.
