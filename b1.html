Mechanical and Electrical Maintenance Services

ARC Facilities supplies mechanical and electrical repairs and maintenance supports. Our competent Mechanical and Electrical team provide repairs, maintenance and proactive planned preventive maintenance of mechanical and electrical systems.

Our mechanical services ranges from the maintenance and repair of both low temperature hot water (LTHW) and high temperature hot water (HTHW) systems. We service, repair and maintain local and central heating systems, ventilation and air-conditioning systems (HVAC), which includes units such as air handing units (AHUs) , fan coil units (FCUs) , split units , package units and extract fans, including accompanying commercial ducting and reheat systems.

Our electrical services include the maintenance, wiring and installation of high and low voltage electrical systems. We service a range of equipments, including generators, UPS, large and small appliances and emergency lighting. We also resolve various power and general lighting conditions and different electrical transmission issues. such as High voltage power supply and distribution. We provide the right documents from authorized persons to assure compliance. We are here to assist.
